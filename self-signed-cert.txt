Generate self-signed PKCS12 cert, password 123456
keytool -genkeypair -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -storetype PKCS12 -keystore mykeystore.p12 -validity 365

List the keystore certs
keytool -v -list -keystore mykeystore.p12 -storepass 123456

Another cert in a truststore
keytool -genkeypair -keyalg RSA -keysize 2048 -storetype jks -keystore abctruststore.jks -validity 365 -alias abckey

Import certificate:
keytool -importkeystore -srckeystore mykeystore.p12 -srcstoretype PKCS12 -destkeystore abctruststore.jks -deststoretype jks
